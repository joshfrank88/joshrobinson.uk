About Me

Hi — I'm Josh. I've spent the last few years building student communities, running events, and helping young people find confidence in a chaotic world.

Whether it's through 1:1 tutoring, school workshops, or live events, I believe every student deserves to feel capable and excited about their future. As a first-class graduate of King's College London in War Studies and History, I understand the challenges students face and the potential they hold.

Through London Student Network and my work with schools and organizations, I've had the privilege of watching thousands of students discover their voice, build their confidence, and create their own path forward.
