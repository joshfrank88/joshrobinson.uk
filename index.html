Hi — I'm Josh.

I'm a speaker, tutor, and educator who's spent the last few years building student communities, running events, and helping young people build confidence in a chaotic world.

I graduated with First-Class Honours in War Studies and History from King's College London, where I also founded the London Student Network — one of London's fastest-growing intercollegiate student startups.

Through 1:1 tutoring, workshops, and live events, I've helped thousands of students find clarity, connection, and confidence.
